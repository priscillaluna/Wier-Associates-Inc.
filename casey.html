Casey York was a very motivated teenager very different than most of his peers. He participated in leadership camps, and served as an officer on local and distrcit levels, along with going to State with his team several years performing FFA Parlimentary Procedures competetions.

Casey started his career here at Wier and Associates as a rodman on a survey crew. Over a two year term, with hard work and determiniation he advanced through the crew ranks to chief is own crew. Casey trained several people over the next few years, of which most of those landed in Party Chief positions as the firms Survey Department was growing.

In 2001, Casey finally made his way into the office working as a survey CAD technician, where he quickly showed interest and started to excel in computers. He brought field to finish type automation for CAD and assisted in bridging the gaps between Surveyors and Engineers. The firm took interest in Casey knowing he was self taught when it came to building PCs and being computer savy in general and asked him to work part time along with the IT Manager.

20 years later 7 work positions later, Casey now is the firms IT Manager and deals with a wide range of equipement and personel.

Casey loves his family, his church, and country life. He strives to serve for his Lord and Savior every chance he gets.
