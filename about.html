About Us

Wier & Associates, Inc. is a consulting firm established in 1978. We provide civil engineering, land surveying, and land planning related services for various types of clients and projects.

Wier & Associates maintains its headquarters in Arlington, Texas and has an East Texas branch office in Henderson, Texas. The firm currently employs 35+ persons, including nine (9) registered as Texas Professional Engineers and four (4) registered as Texas Professional Land Surveyors.

Our staff has extensive on-the-job experience with design, preparation of plans and construction administration on grading, paving, storm drainage, water and sanitary sewer projects. Wier & Associates has been the Engineer/Surveyor/Planner on over 200 million dollars of construction projects for public sector clients alone. We have designed civil improvements for land development projects for private developers in over 60 cities in North Central Texas.
